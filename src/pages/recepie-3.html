Breakfast oats

This recipe combines the wholesome goodness of oats with creamy yogurt, tart frozen raspberries, and nutrient-rich chia seeds for a satisfying and nutritious breakfast or snack option. It's a quick and easy way to fuel your day with fiber, protein, and antioxidants.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup frozen raspberries
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds

Cooking steps

  1. Prepare the oats: In a bowl, add the rolled oats. You can use instant oats if you prefer a quicker cooking time.
  2. Add yogurt: Spoon the plain yogurt over the oats. You can use Greek yogurt for extra creaminess and protein.
  3. Add frozen raspberries: Sprinkle the frozen raspberries over the yogurt and oats mixture. The frozen raspberries will add a refreshing burst of flavor and texture.
  4. Sprinkle with chia seeds: Finish off the dish by sprinkling chia seeds on top. Chia seeds are packed with omega-3 fatty acids, fiber, and protein, making them a nutritious addition to any meal.
  5. Let it sit: Allow the oats, yogurt, raspberries, and chia seeds to sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together and the oats to soften slightly.
  6. Serve and enjoy: Once the oats have softened to your liking, give everything a gentle stir to combine. Enjoy your delicious and nutritious oats with yogurt, frozen raspberries, and chia seeds!
